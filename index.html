Donate to The Heritage Foundation

The Heritage Foundation, America’s most broadly-supported policy organization, effectively communicates rigorous conservative policy research to Congress and the American people.

  • More than 500,000 Americans donate to support Heritage’s work
  • Our more than 100 policy experts and researchers are invited to testify before Congress nearly 40 times a year
  • Heritage experts appear daily in America’s top news outlets—Fox News and The Wall Street Journal, as well as new media outlets like The Daily Signal
  • Heritage provides Members of Congress with proven conservative policy recommendations and recommends the personnel needed to staff their Capitol Hill offices.
  • Located steps from the Capitol, Heritage has promoted principled policy solutions for almost 50 years

Your donation will advance your principles—free enterprise, limited government, individual freedom, traditional American values, and a strong national defense—at a time when our nation needs these principles most.
